# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in VibeSec respectful, harassment-free, and welcoming regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, assume good intent while evaluating evidence, respect privacy, accept correction, and focus criticism on work rather than people. Do not publish private information, credentials, exploit details that create unnecessary risk, or targeted harassment.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md). Maintainers may edit or remove harmful contributions, issue warnings, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, subject to safety and legal obligations.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1 principles.
